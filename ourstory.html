Our Story

We share a passion for esports.
We respect every gamers’ dedication.
And we've got a whole lot of love for the community.
That's why we started Top Dog Esports.

We want to bring gamers together and do our part to help grow the esports community.

Top Dog Esports manages live and online esports events. We’re friendly. We’re fun. We’re safe. And we’re social so everyone stays connected.

We’re always on the hunt for new and exciting places where esports can change the game for players and fans. That’s why you’ll find Top Dog Esports at golf tournaments, music festivals, college campuses and sports arenas.
